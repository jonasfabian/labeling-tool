Recoding/Labeling

After you have registered yourself successfully, you can start the labeling/recoding process.

Labeling

Click the menu-icon in the top left corner to expand the sidenav. After you click on "Check" you can begin with checking already matched snippets

Recoding

Click the menu-icon in the top left corner to expand the sidenav. After you click on "Record" you can begin with recoding excerpts.

In case you are labeling for a group and the excerpt contains sensitive data you can press the private button.

In case the excerpt does not make sense. you can press the "Sentence Error" button
